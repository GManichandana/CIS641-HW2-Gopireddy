Term Project

I have been working on the project Diabetes Risk prediction System. A significant percentage of the world's population is afflicted by diabetes, a condition that is both common and frequently misdiagnosed. By determining whether a certain data point relates to a person with diabetes, this program aims to use data science to prevent diabetes before it occurs. Because the pancreas is unable to create enough insulin or because the body is unable to efficiently use the insulin that is produced for energy conversion, diabetes presents as hyperglycemia, the condition in which blood glucose levels exceed the body's essential threshold. This study uses the classification technique, a subset of supervised machine learning, with a primary focus on the three main kinds of diabetes—Diabetes Mellitus, Diabetes Insipidus, and Gestational Diabetes. It employs a variety of classification methods, such as K-Nearest Neighbors and Support Vector Machine, to methodically construct and improve predictive models.
